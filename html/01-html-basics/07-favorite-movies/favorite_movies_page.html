My Top 5 Favorite Movies

Dhurandhar: The Revenge | Border 2 | Ikkis | Tu Yaa Main | O' Romeo

1. Dhurandhar: The Revenge

Description: This action-thriller is the biggest blockbuster of the year. Directed by Aditya Dhar, it stars Ranveer Singh as an undercover operative in a dangerous criminal underworld.

IMDb: Dhurandhar: The revenge

2. Border 2

Description: A massive hit and a highly anticipated war sequel. The story revisits the sentiments of the 1971 war and stars Sunny Deol alongside new entrants like Varun Dhawan and Diljit Dosanjh.

IMDb: Border 2

3. Ikkis

Description: Directed by Sriram Raghavan, this is an emotional, anti-war film. It follows the life of brave martyr Arun Khetarpal and stars Agastya Nanda alongside veterans like Dharmendra and Jaideep Ahlawat.

IMDb: Ikkis

4. Tu Yaa Main

Description: A thrilling and surprising survival film directed by Bejoy Nambiar. It stars Adarsh Gourav and Shanaya Kapoor and mixes social storytelling with high-stakes tension.

IMDb: Tu Yaa Main

5. O' Romeo

Description: A gritty crime drama directed by Vishal Bhardwaj. Starring Shahid Kapoor and Triptii Dimri, it takes viewers into the underworld of post-independence Mumbai.

IMDb: O' Romeo
